# NQI Software

> **Browse the archive:** https://samuelstein1224.github.io/NQI_Software/

An open, community-curated index of software produced by the five U.S. Department of Energy **National Quantum Information Science Research Centers**, established under the [National Quantum Initiative Act](https://www.congress.gov/bill/115th-congress/house-bill/6227) and renewed in November 2025 with $625M over five years.

| Center | Lead Lab | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| **[C2QA](centers/c2qa.md)** — Co-design Center for Quantum Advantage | Brookhaven National Laboratory | Co-design of materials, devices, software & error correction |
| **[Q-NEXT](centers/q-next.md)** | Argonne National Laboratory / SLAC | Distributed entanglement, quantum networking, foundries |
| **[QSA](centers/qsa.md)** — Quantum Systems Accelerator | Lawrence Berkeley National Lab / Sandia | Neutral atoms, trapped ions, superconducting co-design |
| **[QSC](centers/qsc.md)** — Quantum Science Center | Oak Ridge National Laboratory | Fault-tolerant hybrid quantum-HPC ecosystem |
| **[SQMS](centers/sqms.md)** — Superconducting Quantum Materials & Systems | Fermi National Accelerator Laboratory | SRF-cavity quantum platforms, materials & control |

## Using the archive

The site at the link above provides:

- **Topic-first browsing** — filter by research area (Fault Tolerance, Quantum Networking, Bosonic / Hybrid, Compilation, HPC Simulation, Tensor Networks, Open Quantum Systems, Quantum Sensing, …).
- **Center, type, and status filters** that combine with topics and free-text search.
- **Search aliases** so `qec`, `ft`, or `fault tolerance` all surface the same entries.
- **Request software addition** — paste a GitHub URL; the page fetches the README and metadata, detects NQI center mentions and DOE contract numbers, and opens a structured submission for maintainer review.

## Scope and verification

An entry is **active** when its paper, README, or grant statement explicitly acknowledges one of the five centers. Entries with circumstantial evidence (author affiliation, lab GitHub org) are marked **pending** until acknowledgment is confirmed — see [`centers/_pending.md`](centers/_pending.md) for the queue.
