# C2QA — Co-design Center for Quantum Advantage

**Lead:** Brookhaven National Laboratory.
**Site:** [bnl.gov/quantumcenter](https://www.bnl.gov/quantumcenter/) · **GitHub org:** [github.com/C2QA](https://github.com/C2QA)
**Funding citation:** typically `DE-SC0012704`.

C2QA co-designs materials, devices, software, and error correction with the goal of delivering a 10× improvement in each layer of the stack.

## Software

### [bosonic-qiskit](https://github.com/C2QA/bosonic-qiskit)

Qiskit extension for simulating hybrid boson-qubit systems — cavity-QED-style circuits with combined photonic and discrete-variable degrees of freedom.

- **Type:** simulator (Qiskit extension)
- **Primary institution(s):** Brookhaven National Laboratory + C2QA partners
- **Acknowledgment:** NQI C2QA effort
- **License:** BSD-2-Clause
- **Status:** active

### [nwqec](https://github.com/pnnl/nwqec)

Toolkit for fault-tolerant quantum circuit transpilation and T-count optimization.

- **Type:** benchmark
- **Primary institution(s):** Pacific Northwest National Laboratory
- **Acknowledgment:** C2QA (DE-SC0012704); also QSC — see [qsc.md](qsc.md)
- **License:** MIT
- **Status:** active

### [QASMBench](https://github.com/pnnl/QASMBench)

Low-level OpenQASM benchmark suite for NISQ evaluation and simulation.

- **Type:** benchmark
- **Primary institution(s):** Pacific Northwest National Laboratory
- **Acknowledgment:** Supported by the DOE Office of Science National QIS Research Centers, C2QA
- **Paper:** [arXiv:2005.13018](https://arxiv.org/abs/2005.13018)
- **License:** BSD-3-Clause
- **Status:** active
