# Q-NEXT

**Lead:** Argonne National Laboratory, in partnership with SLAC.
**Site:** [q-next.org](https://q-next.org/)
**Funding citation:** typically `DE-AC02-06CH11357` (Argonne).

Q-NEXT focuses on distributing entanglement across networks, building quantum interconnects, and operating the Argonne and SLAC quantum foundries.

## Software

### [SeQUeNCe](https://github.com/sequence-toolbox/SeQUeNCe)

Discrete-event simulator of QUantum NEtwork Communication — models photonic network components, control protocols, and applications.

- **Type:** quantum-network simulator
- **Primary institution(s):** Argonne National Laboratory; University of Chicago
- **Acknowledgment:** developed under Q-NEXT; contact Caitao Zhan (czhan@anl.gov)
- **Paper:** [Quantum Science and Technology 6 045027 (2021)](https://iopscience.iop.org/article/10.1088/2058-9565/ac22f6) / [arXiv:2009.12000](https://arxiv.org/abs/2009.12000)
- **License:** BSD-3-Clause
- **Status:** active

### [QICK](https://github.com/openquantumhardware/qick) (joint w/ SQMS)

Quantum Instrumentation Control Kit — open-source qubit controller (firmware + Python) for Xilinx RFSoC platforms. The QICK paper acknowledges *both* Q-NEXT and SQMS.

- **Type:** control firmware + software
- **Primary institution(s):** Fermilab + University of Chicago
- **Acknowledgment:** Q-NEXT and SQMS, contract `DE-AC02-07CH11359`
- **Paper:** [arXiv:2110.00557](https://arxiv.org/abs/2110.00557)
- **License:** MIT
- **Status:** active
