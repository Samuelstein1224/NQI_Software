# QSA — Quantum Systems Accelerator

**Lead:** Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory; **lead partner:** Sandia National Laboratories.
**Site:** [quantumsystemsaccelerator.org](https://quantumsystemsaccelerator.org/) · [open-source page](https://quantumsystemsaccelerator.org/open-source-software/)
**Funding citation:** typically `DE-AC02-05CH11231` (LBNL).

QSA co-designs algorithms, devices, and engineering across three platforms — neutral atoms, trapped ions, and superconducting circuits — with 15 partner institutions and 250+ researchers.

## Software

### [QICK-DAWG](https://github.com/sandialabs/qick-dawg)

Defect Arbitrary Waveform Generator — QICK-based control framework for solid-state quantum defects (NV centers, etc.) used in quantum sensing.

- **Type:** control framework / sensing
- **Primary institution(s):** Sandia National Laboratories
- **Acknowledgment:** Sandia (QSA partner); built on SQMS-developed QICK
- **Paper:** [arXiv:2311.18253](https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.18253)
- **License:** see repo
- **Status:** active
